Prospecting

  • Apollo — company and contact discovery with filters.
  • BuiltWith — find sites by tech stack to match domain use cases.
  • Crunchbase — funding and company lists for prioritization.
  • LinkedIn Sales Navigator — people and account targeting.
  • Wappalyzer — alternate to BuiltWith for stack intel.

Research

  • Clearbit — firmographic enrichment to refine fit.
  • Google operators — use site: and intitle: to find likely buyers.
  • Similarweb — traffic and competitors for pitch context.
  • Google Trends — interest signals to time outreach.

Email finding

  • Hunter — domain search and pattern guessing.
  • RocketReach — emails for executives and teams.
  • Snov — finder plus basic outreach.
  • Voila Norbert — simple finder with exports.

Verification

  • Mail-Tester — quick spam score check.
  • MXToolbox — DNS, SPF, DKIM, and blacklist checks.
  • NeverBounce — bulk email verification.
  • ZeroBounce — alternate bulk verifier.

Cold outreach

  • GMass — send from Gmail with mail merge.
  • Instantly — multi-inbox warmup and campaigns.
  • Lemlist — personalization and sequence tooling.
  • Mailshake — proven cold email platform.
  • QuickMail — deliverability focused sending.
  • Woodpecker — simple sequences and replies.

CRM and tracking

  • Airtable — light CRM with views and automations.
  • Notion — notes and pipeline in one place.
  • Pipedrive — sales pipeline for domains.
  • Streak for Gmail — CRM inside your inbox.
  • Zapier / Make — automate leads, sends, and logging.
